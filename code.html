Understanding Your Plan

What is NDIS Plan Management?

NDIS Plan Management is like having a personal bookkeeper for your NDIS funding. It is an option funded by the NDIS that allows you to have a professional manage the financial aspects of your plan.

By choosing PlanWise, we handle the administrative burden of receiving invoices, claiming funds from the NDIS portal, and paying your providers. This means less paperwork for you and more time to focus on what matters most.

  • check_circle No out-of-pocket costs: The fees are covered separately by the NDIS in your 'Improved Life Choices' budget.
  • check_circle Maximum flexibility: Access both registered and non-registered NDIS providers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NDIS Plan Management and how we can help you.

What is plan management? expand_more
Plan management is a service funded by the NDIS that helps you manage the financial and administrative aspects of your NDIS plan. A plan manager acts like a bookkeeper, paying invoices, tracking your budget, and ensuring compliance with NDIS pricing guidelines.
Is plan management free? expand_more
Yes, there are no out-of-pocket costs to you. The NDIS provides separate funding in your plan specifically for plan management under the 'Improved Life Choices' category. This does not take away from your other support budgets.
Can I choose any provider? expand_more
Yes! One of the biggest benefits of using a plan manager like PlanWise is that you can use both NDIS registered and unregistered providers. This gives you much more choice and control over who supports you.
How quickly are invoices paid? expand_more
At PlanWise, we pride ourselves on fast payments. We typically process and pay approved invoices within 24-48 hours of receiving them, ensuring your providers are paid promptly.
Can I switch plan managers? expand_more
Absolutely. You have the right to change your plan manager at any time if you are not satisfied with your current service. The process is simple, and we can help guide you through the transition.
Do you operate Australia-wide? expand_more
Yes, PlanWise provides plan management services to NDIS participants right across Australia, regardless of your location. Our services are fully accessible online and over the phone.
How do I get started? expand_more
Getting started is easy! You can sign up online through our website, call us directly at 1300 CARE, or fill out our contact form and one of our friendly team members will be in touch to assist you.
